Company Policy

Simple policies that keep our community fair, inclusive, and drama-free.

Conduct & Professionalism

  • Respectful behavior is required at all times. Zero tolerance for hate speech or targeted harassment.
  • Keep comms constructive. Disagreements go to DM or a staff ticket.

Brand & Representation

  • Use approved tags, liveries, and callsigns when provided.
  • Do not impersonate staff or speak on behalf of the company without authorization.

Operations

  • Loads and routes should be realistic and safe; no intentional damage or chaos-driving.
  • Convoy leads may adjust plans for safety or server conditions.

Reporting & Appeals

  • Use the #tickets system for reports. Include evidence if available (clips/screenshots).
  • Appeals are reviewed by management within a reasonable timeframe; decisions aim to be consistent and fair.

Data & Privacy

We only collect what we need to run the VTC (e.g., Discord ID, callsign). We do not sell data.
