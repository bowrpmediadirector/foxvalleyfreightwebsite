Employee Handbook

Your quick reference for driver expectations and daily operations.

1) Code of Conduct

  • Treat all players and staff with respect. No harassment, slurs, or trolling.
  • Use clear, calm voice comms. Follow channel etiquette and push-to-talk where required.
  • Represent Trailblazer professionally in-game, on Discord, and on social platforms.

2) Driving Standards

  • Obey posted speed limits and road signs. We prioritize safe, realistic driving.
  • Yield appropriately, use indicators, and maintain safe following distances.
  • No reckless overtakes, brake-checking, or blocking. Respect other VTCs and public drivers.

3) Convoy Protocol

  • Be on time with a fueled, repaired truck. Livery preferred when available.
  • Follow the assigned formation and convoy lead instructions.
  • Keep radio/Discord clear for lead and staff calls during maneuvers.

4) Communication & Tools

  • Discord is required for coordination. Nickname should include your callsign.
  • Use company channels for loads, photos, and scheduling.
  • Game mods should be stable and permitted in event notices.

5) Attendance & Activity

  • Convoys are optional but encouraged. RSVP when possible.
  • If inactive for 30+ days, notify staff to avoid removal.

6) Compliance

  • Follow Discord Community Guidelines in all communications.
  • Adhere to official SCS/Convoy Multiplayer rules while trucking.

7) Discipline

We use a transparent, progressive system: verbal note → written warning → timeout (temp removal from events) → removal/blacklist for severe or repeated issues.

8) Safety First

  • Report crashes or issues via #incident-reports with date/time, location, and brief description.
  • If you feel unsafe in traffic, pull over to a safe shoulder or rest area.
